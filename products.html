QTConnect

QTConnect is a highly secure, end-to-end messenger application available on both mobile devices and desktop. It offers end-to-end encryption, ensuring that all messages, voice calls, and shared media are securely encrypted and only accessible to the sender and intended recipient, providing a high level of privacy. With its robust security features, comprehensive messaging capabilities, and cross-platform support, QTConnect is designed to meet the needs of organizations and users looking for a reliable and private communication solution.

Features

  • Messaging
  • Media Sharing
  • Voice Calls
  • QTConnect Desktop
  • Cross-platform Compatibility
  • End-to-end Encryption

Event Management System

The EMS is a customizable solution tailored to meet unique user needs, supporting the accreditation of participants. Designed for events with 200 to over 2,000 attendees, it provides a centralised, bespoke system for both event planners and attendees.

Features

  • API Integration
  • Post-Event Report
  • Bulk-Mailing
  • Participants Registration and Analytics
  • Badges Design
  • Participants Scanning & Verification

Visitor Management System

VMS is a web and mobile-based application designed to help institutions and companies track, monitor, and manage visitors and employees entering their premises using cutting-edge technology. The system offers a comprehensive solution for both visitor and staff monitoring.

Features

  • Badge Design , Assignment and Printing
  • Employee and Visitor Verification with Entry and Exit Logging
  • System Audit Trails
  • Requirements Gathering
  • Data Import/Export Capabilities
